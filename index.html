Who are you, really?

Reflect. Explore identity and meaning—together.

“The unexamined life is not worth living.” – Socrates Who are we, and how do we become who we are? In this interactive workshop, we explore identity, belonging, and the power of language through discussion and simple exercises. Inspired by key philosophical ideas, we connect these questions to everyday life—no prior knowledge needed. Bring your curiosity, share your thoughts, and discover a new perspective on yourself.
